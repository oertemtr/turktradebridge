Why Choose Turkish Seafood?

Three Seas Advantage

Access to Mediterranean, Black Sea, and Aegean waters. Growing aquaculture in sea bass, sea bream, and trout farms. Cold-water species and warm-water varieties. Diverse sourcing for year-round supply.

EU Processing Standards

EU-approved processing facilities. HACCP certification. BRC standards compliance. Meet strict European safety and quality requirements. Trusted by premium importers across the continent.

Rapid Aquaculture Growth

Sea bass and sea bream farms expanding rapidly. Trout production scaling up. Sustainable farming practices. Consistent supply for growing global demand. Quality comparable to wild-caught.

Strategic Shipping Hub

Fresh fish to Europe in 24-48 hours. Frozen product globally competitive. Turkish Airlines cargo network. Multiple shipping routes. Cold chain infrastructure optimized for seafood.
