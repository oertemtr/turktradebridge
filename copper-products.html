Complete Product Range

Copper Cathodes & Refined Copper

Ultra-pure refined copper products meeting LME specifications. Essential raw material for electrical, electronics, and renewable energy applications worldwide.

  • LME Grade A copper cathodes
  • Electrolytic copper (99.99% purity)
  • Copper billets for drawing
  • Copper ingots
  • Fire-refined copper

Copper Wire & Rod

Specialized wire and rod products for electrical transmission, winding, and automotive applications. High conductivity ensuring minimal power loss in critical applications.

  • Copper wire rod (8mm standard)
  • Drawing quality wire
  • Enameled copper wire
  • Tinned copper wire
  • Copper bus bars
  • Copper strips and foils

Copper Tubes & Pipes

Precision-engineered copper tubing for HVAC, plumbing, refrigeration, and medical applications. Superior corrosion resistance ensuring decades of reliable service.

  • ACR copper tubes for refrigeration
  • Plumbing copper pipes
  • Medical gas copper tubes
  • Copper fittings (elbows, tees, couplings)
  • Capillary tubes

Brass & Copper Alloys

High-performance alloys for automotive, plumbing, electrical, and industrial applications. Wide alloy range optimized for specific mechanical and thermal requirements.

  • Brass rods in multiple grades
  • Brass sheets and plates
  • Brass valves and fittings
  • Bronze products
  • Copper-nickel alloys
  • Beryllium copper
