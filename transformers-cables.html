Complete Product Range

Power Transformers by Type

Comprehensive selection of power transformers tailored for diverse industrial applications. From step-up and step-down configurations to specialized grounding and isolation transformers, our portfolio covers three-phase and single-phase requirements across voltage classes.

  • Step-up transformers for voltage elevation
  • Step-down transformers for voltage reduction
  • Autotransformers for energy-efficient conversion
  • Distribution transformers (pole-mounted, pad-mounted)
  • Instrument transformers (CT, PT)
  • Grounding transformers for system stability
  • Three-phase power transformers
  • Single-phase transformers
  • Isolation transformers for protection

By Design & Cooling Systems

Advanced thermal management solutions ensure optimal performance in demanding environments. Our transformers employ industry-leading cooling technologies including liquid-immersed designs and dry-type configurations for enhanced reliability and efficiency.

  • Liquid-immersed (oil) transformers
  • Dry-type transformers
  • Cast resin transformers
  • ONAN/ONAF cooling systems
  • OFAF/ODAF cooling configurations

By Voltage Classification

Solutions across the complete voltage spectrum ensure compatibility with regional grid standards and industrial requirements, from distribution networks to ultra-high-voltage transmission systems.

  • Low voltage transformers (up to 1kV)
  • Medium voltage systems (1-36kV)
  • High voltage transmission (36-170kV)
  • Extra high voltage (170-800kV)

High-Performance Cable Solutions

Comprehensive cabling systems engineered for reliability, safety, and performance in renewable energy, infrastructure, and industrial applications worldwide.

  • XLPE insulated power cables
  • PVC insulated cables
  • Armoured cables for protection
  • Submarine cables for offshore applications
  • Fire-resistant cables
  • Overhead bare conductors (ACSR, AAC, AAAC)
  • Copper and aluminum conductors
  • Fiber optic cables
  • Control and instrumentation cables

Switchgear & Protection Devices

Integrated protection systems ensuring grid stability, equipment safety, and compliance with international standards for safe power distribution and system management.

  • Circuit breakers for fault protection
  • Disconnectors for safe isolation
  • Surge arresters for overvoltage protection
  • Fuses for short-circuit protection
  • Relay protection panels
  • Ring main units (RMU)
  • Gas-insulated switchgear (GIS)
