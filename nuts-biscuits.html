Complete Product Range

Premium Hazelnuts

Turkey's most famous product, commanding 70% of global hazelnut market. Available in diverse forms for chocolate, confectionery, and culinary applications worldwide.

  • Raw hazelnuts (in-shell, shelled)
  • Roasted hazelnuts with optimal flavor
  • Blanched hazelnuts
  • Hazelnut flour and meal
  • Hazelnut paste and spreads
  • Premium hazelnut oil
  • Chopped, diced, sliced forms
  • Organic certified hazelnuts

Specialty Pistachios

World-renowned Antep pistachios with distinctive flavor and quality. Premium source for confectionery, snacking, and industrial applications globally.

  • Antep pistachios (in-shell, shelled)
  • Roasted and salted varieties
  • Raw pistachio kernels
  • Pistachio paste
  • Pistachio flour
  • Green peeled pistachios

Dried Fruits

Sun-dried premium fruits from ideal growing regions. Natural sweetness preserved for health-conscious and gourmet markets internationally.

  • Dried apricots from Malatya
  • Sultana raisins
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Dried mulberries
  • Dried cherries
